About Me

Developer, Content Creator & Problem Solver

Hi, I'm Osayande (Daniel) Omoregie a developer, content creator, and problem solver passionate about technology and storytelling. Currently studying Computer Science at the University of Houston, I've built solutions ranging from operational software for NASA's International Space Station to web dashboards supporting hundreds of users in education. At NASA, I not only contributed production level code but also took on product management and scrum master responsibilities, setting the long-term vision for the Fire Port auto-scaling project by outlining goals, defining features, and guiding how it would evolve over time. The result was a solution that reduced manual adjustments by more than 90% while improving usability for astronauts.

Outside of engineering, I run a tech and lifestyle YouTube channel that has reached over 320,000 viewers worldwide, where I share insights on internships, coding, and college life. I've also led digital marketing campaigns as Marketing Team Lead for NSBE, driving engagement and growing the chapter's online presence. Whether I'm writing code, managing a product roadmap, or creating content, my goal is always the same: to inspire, connect, and have fun.
